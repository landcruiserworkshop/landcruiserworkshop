About this archive  |  Get in touch
About

The Land Cruiser Workshop is a free archive of Toyota Land Cruiser factory service manuals — the original workshop documentation produced by Toyota for dealership technicians and engineers.

The focus is on the 60 Series (1980–1990) and 80 Series (1990–1998), with coverage spanning chassis & body repair, engines, transmissions, electrical wiring, emissions, and air conditioning.

All manuals are provided for personal research, restoration, and maintenance use.

Free Registration

Downloading manuals requires a free account. There are no catches — no subscription, no payment, no spam. Registration takes under a minute and just requires an email address.

We ask which series you own so we can prioritise which manuals to track down next. You can optionally subscribe to occasional updates when new manuals are added to the archive.

Your data is never sold or shared with third parties. You can delete your account at any time by emailing contact@landcruiserworkshop.com.

Privacy

What we collect: Email address, username, which series you own (optional), mailing list preference, and a log of which manuals you've downloaded. We do not collect your name, location, or any payment information.

How we use it: Your email is used to verify your account and, if you opt in, to send occasional updates about new manuals added to the archive. We do not send marketing emails or share your data with any third party.

Data storage: Account data is stored securely on Cloudflare's infrastructure. Passwords are hashed using PBKDF2 and never stored in plain text. Sessions expire after 30 days.

Your rights: You can update your series interest and mailing list preference at any time via the account menu. To delete your account and all associated data, email contact@landcruiserworkshop.com.
