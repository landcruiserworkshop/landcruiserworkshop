About this archive  |  Get in touch
About

The Land Cruiser Workshop is a free archive of Toyota Land Cruiser factory service manuals — the original workshop documentation produced by Toyota for dealership technicians and engineers.

The focus is on the 60 Series (1980–1990) and 80 Series (1990–1998), with coverage spanning chassis & body repair, engines, transmissions, electrical wiring, emissions, and air conditioning.

All manuals are provided for personal research, restoration, and maintenance use.

Contact

For questions about the archive or its contents, get in touch at:

contact@landcruiserworkshop.com

This is my attempt to fix that.
